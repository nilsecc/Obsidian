
Recovery and data following of catastrophic events. Caused by:
- Natural disasters
- Man-made disasters(terrorist)
- Technological failures(System or cyberattacks)

### Disaster Recovery (DR)

Minimize downtime and dataloss
- backing up data
- replicating systems
- jump to alternate sites or cloud environments

EJ: en un concierto si empieza a llover poner paraguas a las cosas electricas y si se va la luz cambias a una bateria portatil

### Bussiness Continuity (BC)

How to mantain operations during and after disasters. This could involve:
- working from home
- use alternative suppliers
- relocate to temporary office
